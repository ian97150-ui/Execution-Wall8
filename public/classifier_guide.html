The tick layer computes PM trade-print features from Tradier's interval=tick endpoint (04:00-09:30 ET window, fetched in 30-min chunks). Fetched automatically when Tradier key is present.

FieldMeaningThreshold
n= Total tick prints in the PM window High-activity tickers (SOAR) can have 50,000+ prints across full PM
Rate Prints per minute during PM >50/min = +5 score (active algo environment)
<5/min = quiet market
VPIN Order flow toxicity proxy (bulk volume classification). Measures imbalance between buyer/seller volume. >0.55 = +6 score (toxic, informed trading)
<0.20 = −5 score (benign, no directional pressure)
0.20-0.55 = moderate
Buy % / Sell % % of PM volume in up-ticks (buyer aggressor) vs down-ticks (seller aggressor) Sell >65% = sellers dominating PM (+10 score)
Buy >65% = buyers active (−10 score, don't short)
Split 40/60 = contested
LargePrint % % of PM volume in prints >5× average size. Proxy for block trades / institutional activity. >20% = +8 score (institutional blocks printing)
DPproxy % % of PM volume in prints >10× average size. Higher bar for institutional proxy. >25% = +8 score (very large prints, quiet distribution)
PathEff Net PM move / total tick-to-tick path. How directional was the PM move? Close to 1.0 = clean directional move
Close to 0.0 = random walk, no conviction
ScoreΔ Net score adjustment from tick layer (added to v3 base score) Positive = tick data confirms short setup
Negative = tick data contradicts short setup
Tick score adjustments are marked CALIBRATION_v1 — thresholds are initial estimates based on OHLCV backtest analogs, not yet directly validated against 50+ live tick sessions. Use as confirming evidence, not primary gate.
